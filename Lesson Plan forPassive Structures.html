Why You Bought That
Julian Thorne  ·  Lead Consumer Behaviour Analyst  ·  12 April 2026

In the modern boardroom, it is thought that brand loyalty is no longer built — it is engineered. As a behaviour analyst, I observe how e-commerce giants have their algorithms map the subconscious of every teenager.

While it is often claimed that these apps empower the consumer, the reality is more strategic. By using hyper-personalised notifications, companies get the user to feel a sense of 'FOMO'.

Ultimately, it is considered a success when a strategic CEO has the media define what 'essential' means, leaving the idealistic teenager to foot the bill.
